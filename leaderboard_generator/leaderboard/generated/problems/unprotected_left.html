unprotected_left

Unprotected lefts involve taking a left turn across lanes of oncoming traffic that do not have a stop sign or traffic-light to "protect" the left turn. These are one of the most difficult situations to navigate as it requires dealing with oncoming cars taking left turns, right turns, and going straight.
